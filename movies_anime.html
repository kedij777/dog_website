Movies / Anime with dog as main character

101 Dalmatians is a classic Disney film that tells the heartwarming story of a brave group of Dalmatians on a mission to save themselves from the clutches of an evil fur coat-obsessed villain.
"A Dog's Journey" follows the story of a dog named Bailey, who embarks on a journey to protect and comfort his owner's granddaughter throughout her life, while reflecting on his own purpose in life as a loyal and loving companion.
"Benji" is a heartwarming adventure film about a stray dog who helps rescue two children from kidnappers, showcasing the unwavering loyalty and bravery that dogs can possess.
"Bolt" tells the story of a dog who believes he possesses superpowers due to his role as a TV star, and his journey to find his way back to his owner, teaching him the true meaning of companionship and friendship along the way.
"A Goofy Movie" features the lovable Goofy and his son, Max, as they embark on a father-son bonding trip, filled with fun and laughter, highlighting the unique bond between dogs and their owners.
"Hachi - A Dog's Tale" is a heart-wrenching true story of a loyal dog named Hachiko, who patiently waits for his owner's return every day, even after his owner's sudden death, showcasing the depth of love and loyalty that dogs can possess. My Personal Favorite!
"The Secret Life of Pets" offers a hilarious insight into the lives of pets when their owners are away, showcasing their quirky and amusing antics and highlighting the unique personalities that dogs and other pets can have.
"The Tibetan Dog" tells the story of a boy named Tenzing and his dog, who must navigate the challenges of life in a remote mountain village, highlighting the strong bond between dogs and their owners and their resilience in the face of adversity.
"Lady and the Tramp" is a classic romantic tale of two dogs from different worlds, Lady and Tramp, as they embark on a journey filled with adventure and romance, showcasing the power of love and companionship between dogs.
